ABOUT US

In 2024, John Smith and Jane Brown wanted to change their lifestyle and to follow the increasingly popular vegan and vegetarian diet. In searching for local vegan and vegetarian restaurants with seasonal, organic offerings, they noticed that many restaurants specializing in this cuisine rely on produce from major grocery chains that employ inorganic practices like pesticide and Genetically Modified Organism (GMO) use. That is when John and Jane decided to create Flourish, a farm-to-table fine dining restaurant whose roots are based in the organic farms of their Western Pennsylvanian birthplaces. Since then, they have hoped to spread their wings and to reach out to as many customers as possible, showcasing the value of locally-based, organic, and exquisite dining.
