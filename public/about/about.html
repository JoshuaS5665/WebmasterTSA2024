ABOUT US

During the year 2024, our co-founders John Smith and Jane Brown were individuals who sought to pursue a healthier lifestyle by incorporating vegan and vegetarian dishes in their diets. Noticing that many restaurants specializing in this cuisine sourced their produce from major produce retailers who pose insurmountable competition to local farms and who sometimes employ inorganic practices like pesticide and GMO use, they decided to create Flourish, a restaurant that not only provides delicious meals but also develops business connections with farms based exclusively in Western Pennsylvania, where they are from. Since then, they have hoped to spread their wings and to reach out to as many customers as possible, bearing the purpose of showcasing the value of locally-based, organic, and exquisite dining.
