Our Mission

SEASONAL MENUS

At Flourish, it is our mission to allow customers to experience the best of what vegan and vegetarian diets have to offer by providing a diverse array of food options through rotating, seasonal menus.

FRESH INGREDIENTS

The second facet to our business is not only to bolster the dining experience in this manner, but also to work to preserve the environment: we are proud supporters of the farm-to-table process in preparing our meals. We partner with local farms in Western Pennsylvania to purchase organic, plant-based ingredients to construct our seasonal menus.

SUSTAINABLE FARMING

We carefully select farms that implement organic methods, that minimize waste, and that support biodiversity in local ecosystems. All of our chosen farms avoid the use of synthetic fertilizers and pesticides, employ crop rotation practices, and compost organic matter.
